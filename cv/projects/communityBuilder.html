Standards & Tooling for building web sites using IBM communities

I defines a set of standards and techniques to be used by Services Order 2 Cash (O2C) teams when building IBM Community Wiki sites for end user consumption. The goal was for the wiki sites to have a the look and feel of a well designed and organized web site. Adherence to these standards will insure a consistent look and feel across Services O2C communities and deliver an optimal end user experience.

To facilitate this I also wrote the O2C community authoring utility. This utility uses XML, XSL, and XSLT processing to allow the communityâ€™s content to be developed outside the community, within a simple XML document(s), and thereafter generated using XSLT processing. The utility allows for the complexity of the wiki page's HTML to be hidden from the author(s).

The standard and tool manage manage and enable the following:

  • Page headers, menus, & navigation
  • Single or multi column content area (witin a single or multiple rows)
  • Content Widgets
    • Information Box
    • Virtual Business Card
    • Table of Contents
  • Including Dynamic Content
    • Including BMS Data as a Service (DaaS) Content
    • Complex DaaS interactions including Dojo Widgets
    • Including BMS Bot Content
    • Including Really Simple Syndication (RSS) Content
    • Including YouTube or Vimeo Content
    • Including Google Maps Content
